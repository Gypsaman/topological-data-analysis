# TDA Bibliography

A comprehensive bibliography of sources used in this research collection, organized by category. All entries include authors, title, venue, year, and (where available) DOI or arXiv identifier.

---

## Foundational Papers

### Topology and Homology

1. **Betti, E.** "Sopra gli spazi di un numero qualunque di dimensioni." *Annali di Matematica Pura ed Applicata*, 4:140–158, 1871.

2. **Borsuk, K.** "On the imbedding of systems of compacta in simplicial complexes." *Fundamenta Mathematicae*, 35:217–234, 1948.

3. **Gabriel, P.** "Unzerlegbare Darstellungen I." *Manuscripta Mathematica*, 6:71–103, 1972.

### Persistent Homology — Core Papers

4. **Edelsbrunner, H., Letscher, D., Zomorodian, A.** "Topological persistence and simplification." *Discrete & Computational Geometry*, 28(4):511–533, 2002. (The original persistent homology paper.)

5. **Zomorodian, A., Carlsson, G.** "Computing persistent homology." *Discrete & Computational Geometry*, 33(2):249–274, 2005. (Algebraic formulation via graded modules.)

6. **Cohen-Steiner, D., Edelsbrunner, H., Harer, J.** "Stability of persistence diagrams." *Discrete & Computational Geometry*, 37(1):103–120, 2007. (The stability theorem.)

7. **Carlsson, G.** "Topology and data." *Bulletin of the American Mathematical Society*, 46(2):255–308, 2009. (Survey article; excellent introduction.)

8. **Carlsson, G., de Silva, V.** "Zigzag persistence." *Foundations of Computational Mathematics*, 10(4):367–405, 2010.

9. **Carlsson, G., Zomorodian, A.** "The theory of multidimensional persistence." *Discrete & Computational Geometry*, 42(1):71–93, 2009.

10. **Cohen-Steiner, D., Edelsbrunner, H., Harer, J.** "Extending persistence using Poincaré and Lefschetz duality." *Foundations of Computational Mathematics*, 9(1):79–103, 2009. (Extended persistence.)

11. **Chazal, F., Cohen-Steiner, D., Glisse, M., Guibas, L.J., Oudot, S.** "Proximity of persistence modules and their diagrams." *Proceedings of the 25th Annual Symposium on Computational Geometry (SCG)*, pp. 237–246, 2009.

12. **Crawley-Boevey, W.** "Decomposition of pointwise finite-dimensional persistence modules." *Journal of Algebra and Its Applications*, 14(5):1550066, 2015. arXiv:1210.0819, 2012.

13. **Chazal, F., de Silva, V., Glisse, M., Oudot, S.** *The Structure and Stability of Persistence Modules.* SpringerBriefs in Mathematics. Springer, 2016. (Isometry theorem.)

14. **Blumberg, A.J., Lesnick, M.** "Stability of 2-parameter persistent homology." *Foundations of Computational Mathematics*, 24(2), 2022.

### Complexes and Geometry

15. **Edelsbrunner, H., Kirkpatrick, D., Seidel, R.** "On the shape of a set of points in the plane." *IEEE Transactions on Information Theory*, 29(4):551–559, 1983. (Alpha shapes precursor.)

16. **Edelsbrunner, H., Mücke, E.** "Three-dimensional alpha shapes." *ACM Transactions on Graphics*, 13(1):43–72, 1994.

17. **Singh, G., Mémoli, F., Carlsson, G.** "Topological methods for the analysis of high dimensional data sets and 3D object recognition." *Eurographics Symposium on Point-Based Graphics*, pp. 91–100, 2007. (The Mapper algorithm.)
